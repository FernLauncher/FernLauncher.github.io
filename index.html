Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for players who want real control. FernLauncher handles the boring stuff — Java, updates, mods — so you can just play.

☕

Automatic Java

Java 8, 17, and 21 are downloaded and managed for you. No version juggling, no system path headaches.

📦

Mod Browser

Browse, install, and update mods directly from Modrinth and CurseForge without leaving the launcher.

🧩

Modpack Support

One-click install for modpacks from Modrinth and CurseForge, with a manual fallback for everything else.

🗂️

Instance Management

Organize your instances into groups, give them custom icons, and import/export them with ease.

🎨

Resource & Shader Packs

Browse and install resource packs and shader packs directly from the launcher — no manual file juggling.

🔄

Auto-Updater

FernLauncher keeps itself up to date in the background. You'll always be on the latest version without thinking about it.

🌙

Dark / Light / System Theme

Choose between dark, light, or system-matching theme — your launcher, your preference.

🌐

English & French

Fully available in English. French translation is underway at around 20% — contributions welcome on GitHub.

🔑

Microsoft & Offline Accounts

Sign in with your Microsoft account or play offline — both supported without compromise.

All major mod loaders are supported, from the classics to the newest.
